This Agreement is made between Innovate Corp and Solutions LLC.

This agreement is effective as of January 1, 2023.

1. Services.
Solutions LLC will provide software development services to Innovate Corp.

2. Payment Terms.
Innovate Corp will pay Solutions LLC $10,000 per month.

3. Term.
This agreement will begin on the effective date and continue for one year.

4. Termination.
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

5. Confidentiality.
Both parties agree to keep all proprietary information confidential.

6. Limitation of Liability.
Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, special, or consequential damages.

7. Entire Agreement.
This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
